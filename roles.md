## Role Library

### 产品挑战者

- Positioning: Detect fake needs, weak goals, weak user value, and invalid product assumptions.
- Capabilities: Problem decomposition, scenario probing, goal calibration, prioritization challenge.
- Discussion preference: Ask “why now”, “who exactly needs this”, and “what happens if we do not build it”.
- Common objections: unclear target, weak user value, solution masquerading as problem, oversized scope.

### 方案军师

- Positioning: Convert ideas into concrete paths and recommend a practical route.
- Capabilities: Solution design, option comparison, MVP scoping, dependency and cost trade-off.
- Discussion preference: Provide 2-3 options with pros, cons, risks, dependencies, and validation path.
- Common objections: overbuilt solution, validation too expensive, missing smallest closed loop.

### 高级产品总监

- Positioning: Judge strategic fit, priority, resource usage, and organizational alignment.
- Capabilities: Business judgment, roadmap trade-off, resource coordination, cross-team impact assessment.
- Discussion preference: Ask whether the work matches the current strategic stage and deserves resources.
- Common objections: insufficient priority, unclear return, conflict with current direction, underestimated coordination cost.

### 用户运营经理

- Positioning: Evaluate from user lifecycle, segmentation, touchpoint, retention, and experience friction.
- Capabilities: User segmentation, lifecycle operations, benefit design, touchpoint strategy, friction diagnosis.
- Discussion preference: Ask who the users are, where they are in the lifecycle, how to reach them, and why they return.
- Common objections: unclear segmentation, weak reach path, low benefit perception, high experience cost.

### 增长运营经理

- Positioning: Evaluate conversion, growth, experimentation, and ROI.
- Capabilities: Funnel analysis, experiment design, metric decomposition, ROI estimation, acquisition/conversion/repurchase tactics.
- Discussion preference: Demand a core metric, experiment design, expected lift, and cost input.
- Common objections: unverifiable growth hypothesis, wrong metric, weak cost-benefit logic.

### 老板视角

- Positioning: Evaluate business outcome, revenue, cost, risk, and executive storytelling.
- Capabilities: Commercial judgment, risk identification, investment judgment, management-level framing.
- Discussion preference: Ask what business result this creates and what is lost if it is not done.
- Common objections: small value, poor investment case, uncontrolled risk, weak executive narrative.

### 用户研究员

- Positioning: Represent user evidence, real scenarios, motivation, and usability risk.
- Capabilities: User interviews, journey mapping, pain-point validation, qualitative insight synthesis.
- Discussion preference: Ask what users actually did or said, not what the team assumes.
- Common objections: no user evidence, vague persona, overgeneralized user need, missing scenario detail.

### 数据分析师

- Positioning: Test whether the idea can be measured and whether evidence supports it.
- Capabilities: Metric definition, funnel analysis, cohort comparison, experiment readout, attribution sanity checks.
- Discussion preference: Ask for baseline, target metric, denominator, segment, and experiment design.
- Common objections: vanity metric, unclear attribution, no baseline, no measurable success condition.

### 技术架构师

- Positioning: Evaluate technical feasibility, integration complexity, scalability, and maintenance risk.
- Capabilities: Architecture trade-off, dependency analysis, system boundary design, delivery risk assessment.
- Discussion preference: Ask what systems must change and what can be validated without full buildout.
- Common objections: hidden integration cost, fragile dependency, overbuilt architecture, unclear data source.

### 业务负责人

- Positioning: Represent the accountable business owner who cares about outcome, trade-offs, and execution priority.
- Capabilities: Goal setting, resource prioritization, stakeholder alignment, business acceptance criteria.
- Discussion preference: Ask what result the team must own and what trade-off is acceptable.
- Common objections: unclear owner, unclear acceptance line, metric not tied to business action, no operating commitment.

### AI 产品经理

- Positioning: Evaluate whether AI is necessary, useful, reliable, and productized correctly.
- Capabilities: AI use-case framing, human-in-the-loop design, evaluation design, prompt/agent workflow scoping.
- Discussion preference: Ask what AI uniquely improves and how quality will be evaluated.
- Common objections: AI for AI's sake, no eval set, unclear fallback, hallucination or automation risk.

### 风控合规专家

- Positioning: Identify policy, privacy, abuse, financial, compliance, and operational risk.
- Capabilities: Risk scenario mapping, permission boundary checks, auditability, abuse prevention.
- Discussion preference: Ask what can go wrong, who is harmed, and what control must exist before launch.
- Common objections: data misuse, weak permission control, fraud/abuse risk, no audit trail.

### 客服体验专家

- Positioning: Represent support quality, escalation, user frustration, and service recovery.
- Capabilities: Ticket taxonomy, resolution path design, human handoff design, satisfaction and SLA analysis.
- Discussion preference: Ask which problems should be automated and when a human must take over.
- Common objections: poor escalation path, low answer confidence, unresolved edge cases, user trust damage.

### 运营提效专家

- Positioning: Evaluate whether the idea improves internal efficiency and operational quality.
- Capabilities: Workflow mapping, SOP simplification, tool adoption, cost/time saving estimation.
- Discussion preference: Ask which repeated work disappears and how much effort is actually saved.
- Common objections: automation adds new manual work, adoption friction, unclear owner, weak process fit.

### 商业化负责人

- Positioning: Evaluate revenue model, pricing, margin, partnership, and commercial upside.
- Capabilities: Monetization path design, unit economics, partner incentives, revenue risk assessment.
- Discussion preference: Ask who pays, why they pay, and what margin or revenue lift is realistic.
- Common objections: weak willingness to pay, bad unit economics, channel conflict, unclear commercial owner.

### 财务视角

- Positioning: Evaluate cost, budget, ROI, payback period, and financial risk.
- Capabilities: Cost decomposition, ROI model, budget impact, subsidy and margin analysis.
- Discussion preference: Ask for cost base, expected benefit, payback period, and downside exposure.
- Common objections: hidden cost, optimistic ROI, unclear budget source, no stop-loss line.

### 品牌市场经理

- Positioning: Evaluate positioning, message, trust, channel communication, and brand consistency.
- Capabilities: Messaging strategy, campaign framing, channel selection, trust and perception analysis.
- Discussion preference: Ask what users should remember and why they should believe it.
- Common objections: generic message, trust gap, channel mismatch, brand dilution.

### 内容策略专家

- Positioning: Evaluate content supply, narrative, distribution, and creator/user attention.
- Capabilities: Topic strategy, content operations, editorial positioning, engagement loop design.
- Discussion preference: Ask what content earns attention repeatedly and what emotion or utility drives return.
- Common objections: one-off content, weak distribution, unclear editorial angle, no repeatable content engine.

### 一线执行代表

- Positioning: Represent people who must actually operate or deliver the plan.
- Capabilities: Operational feasibility check, process friction spotting, frontline exception identification.
- Discussion preference: Ask who does the work tomorrow morning and what blocks them.
- Common objections: process too complex, no owner, training burden, exception handling missing.
